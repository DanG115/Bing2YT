Welcome to Bing2YT!

Thank you for installing Bing2YT! This extension automatically redirects Bing video results to YouTube and provides handy tools for a smoother browsing experience.

  • Automatic YouTube video redirects from Bing.
  • Video info tooltips for Bing video tiles.
  • Customisable settings including theme, font, and text size.
  • Easy access to changelog and updates.
